Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my club with MF?

To register a new karate club, submit an application form to the MF office along with your club constitution, list of members, and coaching qualifications. Annual registration fees apply. Contact the Secretary General for details.

How can an athlete get licensed?

Athletes must be registered through an affiliated MF club. The club submits athlete registration forms along with medical certificates and passport photos. Annual athlete licenses are issued for the competition season.

When are belt grading examinations?

MF organizes national grading examinations twice a year (usually March and September). Clubs may conduct internal gradings up to 3rd Kyu. Brown belt and above requires a national grading panel.

How do I become a certified coach?

MF offers coaching courses at various levels aligned with WKF standards. Level 1 courses are offered regularly. Contact the Technical Commission for upcoming course dates and requirements.

Can I volunteer at MF events?

Yes! We welcome volunteers at all our events. Contact us via email or phone to register your interest. Volunteers receive training and official event merchandise.

How can I sponsor or partner with MF?

We welcome sponsors and partners who share our passion for sports development. Please send a sponsorship inquiry through the contact form or email us at danielvaati2805@gmail.com for our sponsorship packages.
